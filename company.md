# FlowPulse

**Tagline:** Real-time team energy analytics for remote-first companies.

---

## Problem Statement

Remote teams are burning out in silence. Managers have zero visibility into how their teams actually feel day-to-day. By the time someone submits a resignation letter, it's already too late. Traditional quarterly engagement surveys are slow, generic, and largely ignored — they capture a snapshot from weeks ago, not the reality of right now.

Companies lose an average of $15,000–$25,000 per employee to turnover caused by preventable disengagement. The tools that exist today (Slack polls, Google Forms, annual reviews) weren't built for the async, distributed nature of modern work.

---

## Solution

FlowPulse is a lightweight, AI-powered pulse check platform that passively measures team sentiment and proactively surfaces burnout risks — before they become resignations.

It integrates where teams already work (Slack, Teams, Linear, GitHub) and uses anonymized interaction signals combined with optional 15-second micro check-ins to build a real-time energy map of your organization. No surveillance. No keystroke logging. Just signal.

---

## Key Features

### 🔋 Energy Dashboard
A live heatmap showing team energy levels across squads, departments, and time zones. Spot trends at a glance — who's thriving, who's coasting, who's heading toward a wall.

### ⚡ Micro Check-Ins
One-tap daily prompts that take under 15 seconds. Emoji-based, contextual, and entirely optional. Completion rates average 78% — compared to 23% for traditional surveys.

### 🔍 Burnout Radar
ML-driven early warning system that flags at-risk individuals and teams based on pattern shifts — declining participation, after-hours activity spikes, sentiment drift. Alerts go to managers with suggested interventions, not raw data.

### 🔗 Deep Integrations
Native connectors for Slack, Microsoft Teams, Linear, Jira, GitHub, and Notion. FlowPulse reads the rhythm of work without adding another tab to manage.

### 📊 Trend Reports
Weekly and monthly automated reports with actionable insights. "Engineering velocity is up 12%, but backend squad energy has dropped 3 weeks in a row — here's what changed."

### 🔒 Privacy-First Architecture
All individual data is anonymized and aggregated. Employees control their own visibility. SOC 2 Type II certified. GDPR compliant. No manager can see any single person's raw responses unless that person explicitly opts in.
